Abstract

A reliability architecture (M-Stack) that gives AI systems the ability to know when they're wrong. This whitepaper outlines the design, experiments, and operational recommendations for deployment.

Introduction

The field of AI safety needs straightforward, engineerable systems that improve model reliability without changing model weights. M-Stack provides a layered approach combining uncertainty estimation, calibration, judge models, and gating logic to reduce harmful outputs.
